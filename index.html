AVATARA




Avatāra Incarnation of God In Various Forms... But Does Avatāra only Define the Divine? Or do we have our own Avatāras?? The Shades of You That Has Been, Or Yet Awaits Deep Inside you to be Incarnated??? The Good Avatar and The Bad. The Happy Avatar and The Sad. The REAL AVATAR and The HIDDEN AVATAR. Students' Council Proudly Presents the Theme for EUPHORIA 2020 Avatāra To Be or Not To Be Is The Question Everyone has Different Ideas, Imaginations and Expressions which desires a Stage. Every Artist Urges and Dreams of The Ultimate Stage EUPHORIA And This Year We Celebrate this Urge, this Desire... The Urge and Desire to Break Out your Shackles and Break Free into the most Perfect Avatāra of Yourselves. The True Avatāra of Yourself That the World Deserves to See.. Inspired by Hamlet This Year We Pose a Challenge In Front of Each and Every Student of CRCE "To be Or Not To Be Is The Question" The Question you need to ask yourself this year Are you ready to embark on the Journey The Journey of a Lifetime... The Journey of Transcendence from your Astitva to your Avatāra
